# Release Note — Sprint 14

## New Features & Improvements

- Introducing the Operator feature, accessible from the account page. Users can now apply to become an Operator; applications will be reviewed and approved by Prismax administrators, with an email notification sent upon approval.
- A new VLA page is now available on the homepage, where users can register their robot, upload data across various scenarios (e.g. "Tableware Organization and Storage"), and review their upload history.
- Uploaded data is now automatically quality-checked against PrismaX Robotics Collection Quality Standards to ensure submission integrity; quality check results are reflected in the upload history.
- Improved the Connect Wallet panel layout across all states (connected and disconnected), and added a session-completed screen for mobile users.
- Switched from reCAPTCHA to Cloudflare, improving sign-in accessibility for users in China.


---

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed duplicate queue positions to ensure users are correctly placed in the queue.
- Fixed a security issue where certain arm sessions were exploited to illegitimately accumulate points.
